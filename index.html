About this implementation

This is a complete single-file digital adaptation of Catan core gameplay for local pass-and-play. It includes full board generation, setup placement, dice resource production, robber handling, roads/settlements/cities, development cards (Knight, Road Building, Year of Plenty, Monopoly, and hidden victory points), largest army / longest road scoring, turn order, bank and player trading, and automatic win detection at 10 victory points.
